(vs html/body 100dvh) → any content taller than the phone viewport got centered & CLIPPED with no way to scroll.
Hit the jukebox (Play button cut off the right edge; coverflow `flex:1` made a big gap; bottom clipped), AND the
How-to-Play (956px) + Calibration (858px) overlays. Fixes (all verified at 375×620 via bounding-box measurement,
screenshots time out on the animated bg): (1) `#app` → `height:100dvh`. (2) jukebox.css mobile block ≤640: action
row WRAPS (Play full-width on top, BROWSE/ALL SONGS share row below). (3) jukebox.css mobile block ≤900:
`#view-jukebox` scrolls; `.jukebox` coverflow gets fixed `height:clamp(230px,50vh,330px)` (kills the gap +
flex:1 greed); `touch-action:pan-x` so vertical drag = page scroll, horizontal = change song; How-to + Profile
overlays `align-items:flex-start; overflow-y:auto` with card `margin:auto`; `#calib-screen .calib-wrap`
`max-height:92dvh; overflow-y:auto`. Verified: jukebox scrolls (scrollH>clientH), Play fits & reachable, overlays
scroll. REMAINING mobile polish (audit flagged, not yet done): search inputs should be font-size≥16px to stop
