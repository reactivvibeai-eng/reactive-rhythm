## What you're actually getting (itemize this — it's a lot)

**1. A custom-charted, playable level of YOUR song.**
Your track is analyzed note-by-note (musical onset detection + pitch-contour lane mapping) and hand-tuned into a
real Guitar-Hero-style chart across all 5 lanes — taps, sustains (holds), chords, hazard notes, and gap-fill so
there's never dead air. Charted at **Easy / Medium / Hard** so anyone can play it. The biggest moments of your
song are timed to **combo drops** and **Overdrive** payoffs.

**2. A cinematic 1080p level WORLD built from your reference images.**
This is the headline. From your up-to-5 reference images we generate a **multi-stop cinematic journey** — the
camera travels through **3–4 fully-rendered 1080p environments** as you build your combo, with **matched-frame
travel transitions** between them and a **destination cutaway** at the climax. This is the exact premium pipeline
behind our flagship levels (Alarm Clock Hero, High Seas Showdown, Carnival of Nightmares) — not a static
background. Your character(s) and world star in it.

**3. Reactive "drop" moments wired to your song.**
Combo milestones trigger cinematic cutaways; Overdrive activation fires a synced visual payoff; the backdrop
reacts to your playing in real time. The level *responds* to how well you play it.

**4. A custom play-surface + branding.**
A title card, a level cover, and a wordmark — your level, named and branded. Optionally a guitar skin themed to
your world.

**5. Delivered into the game, forever.**
Your level lands in Reactive Rhythm — playable any time, in your library, with its own leaderboard so you and your
friends compete on *your* level.

## How it's made (the work behind the price)
- **1080p AI cinematic generation** — multiple directed video clips (loops + travels + a destination cutaway),
  not one image. Generated at 1080p so it reads sharp on a big screen (most levels of this kind are 720p).
- **Frame-matched chaining** — each environment is hand-stitched frame-to-frame so the journey flows as one
  continuous shot, no jarring cuts.
- **Custom charting + difficulty tuning** — hours of design per song so the notes actually feel like the music
  at every difficulty.
- **Branding + integration** — title card, cover, wordmark, character placement, and wiring it live in-game.
